SEKOU DABO DATA SCIENTIST AND AI ENGINEER

“To progress, it involves accepting failures, making mistakes, while ensuring not to repeat them more than once.”

Hi, I'm Sékou Dabo

I am currently a final-year Master's student in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science at Mundiapolis University in Casablanca, Morocco. My academic journey has fueled my passion for data analysis, with a focus on techniques such as Principal Component Analysis (PCA) and Multiple Correspondence Analysis (MCA). These methods not only uncover valuable insights within data but also significantly simplify the complexity of machine learning algorithms, facilitating better generalization.
I have also delved into the realm of deep learning, working with various neural network architectures, including convolutional, recurrent, and transformer networks. I have explored the application of these skills in exciting domains, such as medical diagnosis through image analysis (convolutional networks) and the creation of innovative molecules (recurrent networks) for disease treatment. Lastly, I am intrigued by the question of which architecture, whether deep or wide networks, is more effective in solving a given task. This question may serve as a guiding theme for my future research.
